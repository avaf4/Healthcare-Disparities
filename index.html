Disparities in Healthcare

Healthcare is an important aspect to everyone's life, thus it should be accessible to all.

Healthcare, most specfically in the United States, can be very costly. This is often explained due to the micro-costs that matriculate during a hospital visit; however, even common healthcare can be tricky to access. The costs, locations, and biases work together to prevent paitents from receiving care.

Take Action!

Become informed! Healthy People is a great resource for becoming aware on the issue and becoming involved.

Support the single-payer movement! Physicians for a National Health Program is a large advocate group that supports the formation of a public insurance plan that works with private medical centers to support all U.S. citizens!
